BOARDS TIP OF THE WEEK
 
 
Finding the Keyboard in Android
 
After a recent Android update, Android users might have difficulty finding the Boards keyboard in their phone. To fix this:
• Open your settings
• Type Manage Keyboard in the search bar
• Press Keyboard Location
• Press Default
• Make sure Input method quick switch is toggled on
After completing those steps, you should see the mini keyboard icon when you want to navigate to the Purium Offical Board. To see this process in action watch the video by clicking below.
